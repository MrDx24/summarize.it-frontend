Tutorial:

Welcome to our website!! To start with the extractive text summarization please follow the below steps:

Step 1:
Select the type of input you want from the options available by clicking one of the following buttons: Text, Url or File.

Step 2:
After clicking on the button you will have an option of pasting text/Url in the given “Input:” section or choosing the file from your desktop. Give the required input.

Step 3:
Now click on the Summarize button on the right to generate the summary.

Step 4:
The summary is generated in the “Output summary:” section and the time required to generate the summary is also displayed.

Step 5:
You can also store the output summary details in pdf file format by clicking the
Save as PDF button.

Step 6:
After clicking on Save as PDF button, pdf is generated with following parameters: given input, summarization method selected, Date & Time and output summary.

Congrats!! You have successfully summarized the text.
